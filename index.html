About Me

I am Saqib Mushtaq Shah, a mathematics student interested in operator algebras, noncommutative geometry and abstract harmonic analysis.

My work focuses on operator algebras arising form combinatorial and algebric structures, in particular directed graphs, groups, semigroups and groupoids, and their connection to abstract harmonic analysis.
